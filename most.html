Adolescence

Adolescence is a crucial and sensitive phase of life that marks the transition from childhood to adulthood. During this time, individuals experience significant physical, emotional, and mental changes. It is a period of self-discovery, confusion, and growing independence. Teenagers often face challenges related to identity, friendships, peer pressure, and future goals. With proper guidance and support, adolescence can become a strong foundation for personal growth and maturity.
